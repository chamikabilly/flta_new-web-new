TENNIS SUMMER CAMP

Check out our unique tennis summer camp in France, UK and Vietnam. Our facilities at the Arcachon Headquarters are one of the most beautiful tennis clubs in France located 200m from the beach, with 22 training grounds (10 clay courts, 10 hard courts, 3 walls, 3 courts outdoor and 4 hard covered courtyards). FLTA welcomes players all year round with bar services, restaurants, 3 rooms (including television, table tennis, marbles …). A gym is a place for club members to swap equipment as well as a professional store equipped with the latest collections.
